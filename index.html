Two Listing Modes

Built for how students
actually live.

Open Room

Slip into
an existing house.

A current tenant posts a vacancy. You apply. If they approve you, you're in. No lease drama, no convincing a full house to start over — just a room that's ready.

before you sign.

A landlord posts an available unit. Students band together as a group and apply jointly for the lease. You choose your housemates first — then find the house together.
